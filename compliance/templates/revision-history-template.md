## Revision Type Definitions

**Major Revision:** Substantive changes to procedure, scope, roles, or compliance requirements. Requires full re-review and re-signature workflow. Version increments major digit (e.g., v1.2 to v2.0). Previous version is marked Superseded.

**Minor Revision:** Clarifications, formatting, typo corrections, non-substantive updates. Owner-approved. Version increments minor digit (e.g., v1.2 to v1.3). Approving authority is notified but does not need to re-sign.
