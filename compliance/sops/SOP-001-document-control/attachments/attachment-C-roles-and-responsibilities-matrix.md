## Notes

1. For clinical SOPs, the Approving Authority (Covering Physician) holds ultimate accountability for clinical content. The Owner is responsible for process management and logistics.
2. Where both Owner and Approving Authority are marked "A," the Owner is accountable for the process and the Approving Authority is accountable for the clinical/professional judgment.
3. "Affected Personnel" includes all employees, contractors, and agents whose work is governed by the specific SOP in question.
4. Reviewers (SME) are engaged only when the SOP subject matter requires external expertise (legal, compliance, pharmacy, etc.). Not all SOPs require SME review beyond the covering physician.
