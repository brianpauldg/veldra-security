## Overview

This matrix consolidates the eligibility criteria for all active Bloom Metabolics service lines into a single at-a-glance reference. The Covering Physician uses this matrix during eligibility screening to quickly assess whether a prospective patient meets, fails, or requires further evaluation against each criterion.

Cells marked `[CLINICAL — PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN]` must be populated by the Covering Physician upon assignment. The structural scaffolding and industry-typical reference points are provided to assist the physician in efficient population.

## How to Use This Matrix

1. Identify the patient's requested service line
2. Review each row in the applicable column
3. For each criterion: confirm met, identify not met (decline), or flag for further evaluation
4. Document the assessment in OptiMantra per SOP-003 §5.8

---

## Maintenance

This matrix is updated whenever the Covering Physician modifies the eligibility criteria in SOP-003 §5.3 through §5.6. Changes to this matrix constitute a minor or major revision of SOP-003 per [SOP-001 §5.11](../../SOP-001-document-control/SOP-001-document-control.md).
