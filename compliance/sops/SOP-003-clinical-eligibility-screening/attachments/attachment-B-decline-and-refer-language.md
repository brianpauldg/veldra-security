## Overview

This attachment provides the approved patient-facing language for decline and refer-out communications. Both operational declines (issued under SOP-002) and clinical declines (issued under SOP-003) use this single source of language to ensure consistency and prevent clinical-conversation drift.

MSO staff delivering these communications use the language verbatim. They do not improvise, expand, or provide clinical explanations beyond what is written here. If the patient has clinical questions about a decline, the MSO Owner escalates to the Covering Physician per SOP-003 §5.9.

---

## Section 1 — Operational Decline Language (Issued Under SOP-002)

These declines are made by MSO operational gates. No clinical judgment is involved.

### Geographic Ineligibility

**Portal/Email:**
> Thank you for your interest in Bloom Metabolics. At this time, we provide telehealth services exclusively to residents of California. We are unable to proceed with your intake.
>
> If your residency status changes in the future, we welcome you to reapply. We wish you the best in finding the care you need.

**Phone (scripted):**
"Thank you for reaching out to Bloom Metabolics. Unfortunately, we're currently only able to serve patients who are California residents. I'm not able to proceed with your intake at this time. If that changes in the future, we'd be happy to hear from you again."

### Age Ineligibility

**Portal/Email:**
> Thank you for your interest in Bloom Metabolics. Based on the information provided, you do not meet the minimum age requirement for the requested service at this time.
>
> If you believe this is an error, or if you have questions, please contact us at [support email].

**Phone (scripted):**
"Thank you for reaching out. Based on the age information in your intake, you don't currently meet the minimum age for the service you're interested in. If you think there's been an error, I can help you look into it."

### Identity Verification Failure

**Portal/Email:**
> We were unable to verify your identity from the documentation provided. This verification is a required step in our intake process.
>
> If you would like to resubmit identification, please contact us at [support email] and we will provide instructions. If you have questions about acceptable identification, we are happy to assist.

**Phone (scripted):**
"We weren't able to verify your identity from the documents you uploaded. This is a required step before we can move forward. I can help you understand what's needed if you'd like to resubmit."

### Payment Failure (After Retry Exhaustion)

**Portal/Email:**
> We were unable to process your payment for the initial clinical evaluation. Your intake is currently on hold.
>
> If you would like to proceed, please contact us at [support email] to resolve the payment issue. We are happy to assist.

## Section 2 — Clinical Decline Language (Issued Under SOP-003)

These declines are made by the Covering Physician based on clinical eligibility review. The language below is the approved patient-facing communication. The clinical reasoning is documented internally in OptiMantra but is NOT included in the patient-facing communication (the patient may request their records, which would include the clinical reasoning).

### General Clinical Decline (Service-Line-Specific)

`[CLINICAL — PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN: Draft and approve the standard clinical decline language for each service line. The language should:

1. Acknowledge that the patient completed the intake process
2. State that the Medical Director has reviewed their information
3. State that the Medical Director has determined that the requested service is not appropriate for them at this time
4. NOT disclose the specific contraindication in the patient-facing message (the patient may request records)
5. Recommend that the patient discuss the matter with their primary care provider
6. Offer to answer questions through a direct physician communication if the patient requests

Suggested template for physician to customize:

"Thank you for completing the intake process with Bloom Metabolics. After reviewing your medical information, our Medical Director has determined that [Service Line] is not appropriate for you at this time. We recommend discussing your health goals with your primary care provider, who can provide personalized guidance. If you have questions about this determination, please contact us and we can arrange a follow-up communication with our Medical Director."]`

### Clinical Decline — Patient Refuses Required Monitoring

`[CLINICAL — PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN: Draft language for patients who decline required monitoring (e.g., refuse lab work). Suggested:

"Bloom Metabolics requires ongoing laboratory monitoring as part of our [Service Line] program. Because you have indicated that you are unable or unwilling to participate in the required monitoring, we are unable to proceed with treatment at this time. If your circumstances change, we welcome you to reapply."]`

## Section 3 — Refer-Out Language (Issued Under SOP-003)

`[CLINICAL — PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN: Draft and approve refer-out language for common referral scenarios. The language should:

1. Acknowledge the patient's intake and the Medical Director's review
2. Explain that the patient's situation would benefit from evaluation by a specialist
3. Recommend a referral category (not a specific provider, unless the physician chooses to)
4. Maintain a supportive tone — a referral is not a rejection

Common referral categories to address:
- Reproductive endocrinology (for patients with fertility goals incompatible with TRT)
- Psychiatry (for patients with active psychiatric conditions requiring stabilization)
- Cardiology (for patients with cardiovascular risk requiring clearance)
- Urology (for patients with prostate concerns requiring evaluation)
- Oncology (for patients with cancer history requiring specialist input)
- Primary care (for patients who need a PCP relationship before telehealth specialty care)
- Emergency services (for patients presenting with acute conditions)

Suggested template:

"Thank you for completing the intake process with Bloom Metabolics. After reviewing your medical information, our Medical Director believes that your situation would benefit from evaluation by a [specialist type] before we proceed with [Service Line]. We recommend contacting your primary care provider for a referral, or you may reach out to [general resource]. This is not a reflection on your eligibility for future care with us — once the recommended evaluation is complete, we welcome you to reapply."]`

## Language Maintenance

All language in this attachment is reviewed and approved by the Covering Physician. Changes to clinical decline or refer-out language require Covering Physician approval and constitute a minor revision of SOP-003.

Changes to operational decline language (Section 1) may be initiated by the MSO Owner but must be reviewed by the Covering Physician to ensure no clinical implications.
