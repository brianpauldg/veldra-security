**Parent Document:** [SOP-005 — Synchronous Telehealth Visit](../SOP-005-synchronous-telehealth-visit.md)

## Overview

This template defines the required documentation structure for the Good Faith Examination (GFE) conducted during a synchronous telehealth visit. It is designed for use within the OptiMantra EHR and constitutes the medico-legal record of the clinical encounter.

California Business and Professions Code Section 2242 requires that a prescriber conduct an appropriate prior examination before prescribing, dispensing, or furnishing a dangerous drug or device. For telehealth encounters, this examination must be conducted via synchronous audio-video technology and must meet the same standard of care as an equivalent in-person examination. This template operationalizes that requirement.

Sections marked `[CLINICAL -- PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN]` require the Covering Physician's clinical judgment to populate with service-line-specific content. The operational scaffolding and structural elements are provided by the MSO to facilitate efficient and consistent documentation.

## 3. History of Present Illness

Document the following for the patient's chief complaint:

- **Onset:** When did the symptoms begin or when did the patient first seek this type of treatment?
- **Duration:** How long have symptoms been present?
- **Severity:** Patient-reported severity and functional impact.
- **Associated symptoms:** Related symptoms reported by the patient.
- **Prior treatments tried:** Medications, lifestyle modifications, or other interventions previously attempted, with outcomes.
- **Impact on daily life:** Occupational, relational, and quality-of-life effects described by the patient.

### Service-Line-Specific HPI Elements

`[CLINICAL -- PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN]`

*The Covering Physician should specify the HPI elements required per service line. Typical examples for reference:*

| Service Line | Typical HPI Elements |
| --- | --- |
| **TRT** | *Symptom inventory (e.g., ADAM questionnaire or AMS scale score); libido, energy, mood, cognitive function, body composition changes; prior testosterone use if any; time of day symptoms are worst* |
| **GLP-1/GIP** | *Weight history (highest weight, current weight, goal weight); prior weight-loss attempts (dietary, pharmacologic, surgical); comorbidities related to obesity; eating patterns; physical activity level* |
| **Sexual Health** | *Onset and pattern (situational vs. global); relationship context and impact; prior use of PDE5 inhibitors or other treatments; cardiovascular symptom screening* |
| **Longevity** | *Specific optimization goals (energy, cognition, recovery, sleep); current performance baseline; prior peptide or supplement use; measurable outcomes the patient is tracking* |
