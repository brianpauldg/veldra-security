## Mental Health Crisis

| Resource | Contact | Hours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** | **Call or text 988** | 24/7 |
| Crisis Text Line | Text **HOME** to **741741** | 24/7 |
| California Peer-Run Warm Line | `[OPERATIONAL — PENDING: verify current number — past number was 855-845-7415]` | Peer-run, hours TBD |

The 988 Lifeline is the first-line national resource for mental health crisis. You can call or text. You do not have to be in a life-threatening crisis to use 988 — it is also for emotional distress, anxiety, or any situation where you need to talk to someone trained to help.

Crisis Text Line is a text-only option for people who cannot safely speak on the phone.

---

## Medical Emergency

| Resource | Contact |
| --- | --- |
| **911** | **Call 911** for any medical emergency or imminent danger to yourself or others |

Call 911 for chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe allergic reaction, signs of stroke (sudden facial droop, arm weakness, speech changes), seizure, severe injury, unconsciousness, or any other situation that feels life-threatening.

If you are in a synchronous video visit with Bloom when an emergency arises, your clinician will help arrange 911 dispatch and will remain on the line.

## Substance Abuse

| Resource | Contact | Hours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SAMHSA National Helpline** | **1-800-662-4357 (HELP)** | 24/7 |
| SAMHSA Online Treatment Locator | findtreatment.gov | Web-based |

The SAMHSA helpline provides free, confidential, 24/7 referrals to treatment for substance use disorders. They do not provide counseling themselves; they connect you to providers in your area.

## Domestic Violence

| Resource | Contact | Hours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **National Domestic Violence Hotline** | **1-800-799-7233** | 24/7 |
| Text option | Text **START** to **88788** | 24/7 |

If you are in immediate danger from violence, call 911 first.

## Veterans

| Resource | Contact | Hours |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Veterans Crisis Line** | **988, then press 1** | 24/7 |
| Text option | Text **838255** | 24/7 |

The Veterans Crisis Line is staffed by people who understand military service. You do not need to be enrolled in VA health care to use it.

## Bloom Metabolics

Bloom Metabolics is your metabolic and endocrine care provider. We do **not** provide emergency, mental health, primary care, or crisis intervention services. For Bloom-specific clinical questions during business hours, message us through the patient portal.

If you are experiencing a crisis, please use the resources above. If you contact Bloom during a crisis through the patient portal, our team will help direct you to appropriate resources, but we are not equipped to provide crisis intervention directly.
