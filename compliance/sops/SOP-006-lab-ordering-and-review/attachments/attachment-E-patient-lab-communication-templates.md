# Attachment E — Patient Lab Communication Templates

**Parent Document:** [SOP-006 — Lab Ordering & Review](../SOP-006-lab-ordering-and-review.md)
**Ownership:** Operational — MSO Owner (drafted); Covering Physician (approves clinical-adjacent language)

---

## Overview

This attachment provides the approved patient-facing communication templates for all lab-related touchpoints. MSO staff use these templates verbatim. Clinical interpretation is never included in operational communications — the Covering Physician communicates clinical findings directly per SOP-006 §5.5.

`[CLINICAL — PENDING COVERING PHYSICIAN: Review and approve all template language below for clinical accuracy and tone. Particular attention to abnormal results language to ensure it does not inadvertently provide clinical interpretation.]`

---

## Template 1 — Lab Order Notification

**Channel:** OptiMantra portal message
**Trigger:** Lab order generated

> **Subject:** Your Lab Work Has Been Ordered
>
> Hi [Patient Name],
>
> Your physician has ordered lab work as part of your care with Bloom Metabolics. Here is what you need to know:
>
> **Tests ordered:** [Panel name or list of tests]
>
> **Where to go:** Any LabCorp Patient Service Center in California. Find a location near you: https://www.labcorp.com/labs-and-appointments
>
> **What to bring:** Your LabCorp requisition (attached) and a government-issued photo ID.
>
> **Before your draw:**
> - Fast for 8-12 hours (water is fine)
> - Stay hydrated the day before and the morning of your draw
> - Take your usual medications unless your physician has told you to hold a specific one
>
> **Scheduling:** You can walk in or schedule online at LabCorp's website. Morning appointments are recommended for fasting labs.
>
> **Cost:** Your lab work is included in your Bloom Metabolics service. You will not receive a separate bill from LabCorp.
>
> If you have questions about scheduling or the draw process, reply to this message or contact us at [support email].
>
> Bloom Metabolics Care Team

## Template 2 — Draw Reminder (3-Day)

**Channel:** OptiMantra portal message or SMS
**Trigger:** 3 days after lab order if no draw completed

> Hi [Patient Name], this is a reminder that your lab work for Bloom Metabolics is ready to be completed at any LabCorp location. Your requisition is in your patient portal. Completing your labs promptly helps us move forward with your care. Questions? Reply here or contact [support email].

## Template 3 — Draw Reminder (7-Day)

**Channel:** OptiMantra portal message
**Trigger:** 7 days after lab order if no draw completed

> Hi [Patient Name], we notice your lab work has not been completed yet. Your physician needs these results to proceed with your evaluation. Please schedule your draw at a LabCorp near you at your earliest convenience. If you are having difficulty scheduling or have questions, please contact us at [support email] and we will help.

## Template 4 — Normal Results Notification

**Channel:** OptiMantra portal message
**Trigger:** Physician acknowledges results as normal

> **Subject:** Your Lab Results Are In
>
> Hi [Patient Name],
>
> Your recent lab results have been reviewed by your physician. Your results are within the expected range.
>
> You can view your full results in your patient portal under Lab Results.
>
> If you have questions about your results, your physician will be happy to discuss them at your next visit. You can also send a message through the portal.
>
> Bloom Metabolics Care Team

---

## Template 5 — Abnormal Results — Physician Will Follow Up

**Channel:** OptiMantra portal message
**Trigger:** Physician flags results as abnormal-actionable and will communicate directly

> **Subject:** Your Lab Results — Follow-Up Needed
>
> Hi [Patient Name],
>
> Your recent lab results have been reviewed by your physician. Some values require further discussion. Your physician will be reaching out to you directly within the next 1-2 business days to review the findings and discuss next steps.
>
> There is no immediate action needed on your part. If you have urgent concerns in the meantime, please contact us at [support email] or call [clinic phone].
>
> Bloom Metabolics Care Team

**Note:** This template does NOT include any specific lab values, interpretations, or clinical recommendations. The physician communicates the clinical details directly.
