## Overview

This attachment defines the required pre-visit laboratory panels for each Bloom Metabolics service line. Pre-visit labs are ordered before the SOP-003 eligibility determination and/or the SOP-005 synchronous telehealth visit. The Covering Physician sets all clinical content; industry-typical references are provided as scaffolding.
