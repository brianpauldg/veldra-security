## Overview

This attachment defines the required post-visit monitoring lab cadence for each Bloom Metabolics service line by treatment phase (initiation, titration, maintenance). Monitoring labs are ordered after the SOP-005 synchronous telehealth visit and feed into future Prescribing SOPs for dose adjustment and treatment continuation decisions.
