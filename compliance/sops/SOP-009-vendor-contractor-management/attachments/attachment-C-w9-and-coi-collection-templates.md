## Purpose

This attachment provides email templates and procedural guidance for collecting required vendor documentation: IRS Form W-9, Certificates of Insurance (COI), and HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAA). These templates are used by the MSO Owner or bookkeeper when onboarding a new vendor per [Attachment B](attachment-B-vendor-onboarding-checklist.md).

---

## Template 1 — W-9 Request Email

**Use when:** Every new vendor engagement, before first payment. No exceptions.

---

**Subject:** Bloom Metabolics — W-9 Request (Required Before Payment)

Dear [Vendor Name],

Thank you for your upcoming engagement with Bloom Metabolics LLC. Before we can process your first payment, we are required to have a completed IRS Form W-9 on file.

**What we need:**
- A completed and signed IRS Form W-9 (Request for Taxpayer Identification Number and Certification)
- The current version of the form is available here: https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-w-9

**Instructions:**
1. Download the form from the IRS link above (or use the attached blank copy)
2. Complete all applicable fields, including:
   - Line 1: Legal name (as shown on your income tax return)
   - Line 2: Business name / DBA (if different from Line 1)
   - Line 3: Federal tax classification (check the appropriate box — individual, LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp, etc.)
   - Part I: Taxpayer Identification Number (SSN or EIN)
   - Part II: Certification — sign and date
3. Return the completed form using one of the secure methods listed below

**Secure return methods (in order of preference):**
- Encrypted email (if your email provider supports encryption)
- Password-protected PDF via email (share the password in a separate message or phone call)
- Secure file upload link: `[OPERATIONAL — TO BE CONFIGURED — e.g., Google Drive shared folder, secure form, or document portal]`

**Important:** Please do NOT send your W-9 as an unencrypted attachment in a standard email if it contains your Social Security Number. Use a password-protected PDF or one of the secure methods above.

**Deadline:** We need your completed W-9 before [DATE — typically engagement start date or first invoice date]. Payment cannot be processed without a W-9 on file.

If you have any questions about completing the form, please contact me directly.

Best regards,
Brian DeGuzman
MSO Owner, Bloom Metabolics LLC

## Template 3 — Certificate of Insurance (COI) Request Email

**Use when:** Vendor's scope of work requires COI per [Attachment B](attachment-B-vendor-onboarding-checklist.md) Item 4 — on-site work, professional services with E&O exposure, or vendors creating third-party liability risk.

---

**Subject:** Bloom Metabolics — Certificate of Insurance Request

Dear [Vendor Name],

As part of our vendor onboarding process, we require a Certificate of Insurance (COI) before your engagement begins. This requirement applies to vendors whose scope of work involves [on-site work at Bloom-controlled locations / professional services with errors-and-omissions exposure / work that creates third-party liability risk — customize as applicable].

**COI Requirements:**

| Coverage Type | Minimum Limit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Commercial General Liability | $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate | Required for all vendors needing COI |
| Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions) | $1,000,000 per occurrence | Required for professional service providers (consultants, accountants, attorneys, developers) |
| Workers' Compensation | Per state statutory requirements | Required if vendor has employees performing work for Bloom |
| Cyber Liability / Technology E&O | $1,000,000 per occurrence | Required for vendors with access to Bloom's digital systems or data |

**Certificate holder information:**

Bloom Metabolics LLC
[BUSINESS ADDRESS]

**Additional requirements:**
- Bloom Metabolics LLC should be listed as an Additional Insured (or Certificate Holder, depending on your carrier's terminology)
- Policy must be current and coverage must extend through the anticipated engagement period
- If the engagement extends beyond the policy expiration, an updated COI is required upon renewal

**How to provide:** Please have your insurance carrier send the COI directly to [EMAIL], or forward a carrier-issued COI PDF. Self-prepared COIs are not accepted.

**Deadline:** We need the COI before [ENGAGEMENT START DATE].

Please contact me if you have questions about these requirements.

Best regards,
Brian DeGuzman
MSO Owner, Bloom Metabolics LLC

**Use when:** Vendor will create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI on Bloom's behalf. Examples: bookkeeper with access to patient billing data, contractor developers with OptiMantra access, scribes, VAs with access to patient records, marketing contractors handling patient testimonials.

## Acceptable Secure Return Methods

The following methods are acceptable for vendors returning sensitive documents (W-9, signed agreements, BAAs):

| Method | Acceptable? | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Password-protected PDF via email (password shared separately) | Yes — preferred | Most practical for most vendors; password must be communicated via separate channel (phone, text, separate email) |
| Encrypted email (vendor's email supports encryption) | Yes | S/MIME or PGP-encrypted email |
| Secure file upload (Bloom-provided link) | Yes — preferred for recurring vendors | `[OPERATIONAL — TO BE CONFIGURED]` |
| Physical mail (USPS, FedEx, UPS) | Yes — last resort | Slower; use only when digital methods are not available |
| Standard unencrypted email attachment | **Not recommended for W-9 with SSN** | Acceptable for COIs, BAAs, and W-9s that contain EIN only (no SSN). NOT recommended for W-9s containing Social Security Numbers |
| Fax | No | Bloom does not operate a fax line at v0.1 |
| Text message / SMS | No | Not secure; not acceptable for any sensitive document |

## Document Handling After Receipt

Upon receipt of any vendor document:

1. **Verify completeness.** The bookkeeper or MSO Owner confirms the document is fully completed and signed. Incomplete documents are returned to the vendor with a specific request for the missing information.
2. **File immediately.** The document is filed in the vendor file at `compliance/vendor-files/[vendor-name]/` within 1 business day of receipt.
3. **Update the onboarding checklist.** The corresponding item on [Attachment B](attachment-B-vendor-onboarding-checklist.md) is marked complete with date and initials.
4. **Secure storage.** W-9s containing SSNs are stored in access-controlled folders. The bookkeeper and MSO Owner are the only personnel with access to vendor W-9s at v0.1.
