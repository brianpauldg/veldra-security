## Category: Pre-Launch

**Is Bloom Metabolics currently accepting patients?**
Not yet. Bloom is in pre-launch. We are building the clinical infrastructure and finalizing our physician partnership. Enrollment opens early-to-mid June 2026. Join the waitlist to be notified.

**When does enrollment open?**
Early-to-mid June 2026. Waitlist members are notified first.

**What does the waitlist do?**
You receive a 3-email welcome sequence introducing the clinical approach, what Bloom offers, and how enrollment works. When enrollment opens, you're notified before the general public. No spam. Unsubscribe anytime.

**When will I be notified?**
Before enrollment opens. You'll receive an email from brian@bloommetabolics.com with instructions on how to book your physician evaluation.

**Do I need to pay anything to join the waitlist?**
No. The waitlist is free. No payment information is collected. The $49 physician evaluation fee applies only when enrollment opens and you choose to book.

**Can I cancel my waitlist signup?**
Yes. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. No follow-up if you opt out.

**What information is Bloom collecting at this stage?**
Email address and optional preference data (first name, area of interest, how you found us). No health information, no medical history, no protected health information (PHI) of any kind. Bloom does not collect PHI during pre-launch.

**Who can I contact with questions?**
Brian DeGuzman, RN — brian@bloommetabolics.com. Reply to any waitlist email or email directly. I read and respond to every message personally.
