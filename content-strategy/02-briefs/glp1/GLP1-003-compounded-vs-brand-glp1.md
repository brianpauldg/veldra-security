## The Angle
This is the article most providers will not write because the regulatory nuance is uncomfortable. Compounded GLP-1 medications exist in a specific legal framework that most marketing content either ignores entirely or oversimplifies to the point of inaccuracy. Bloom writes this piece because patients deserve the full picture — including the parts that complicate the sales pitch.
