## The Angle
The clinical data on weight regain after GLP-1 discontinuation is significant — the STEP 1 extension study showed approximately 67% of weight loss was regained within one year of stopping. Most providers do not discuss this upfront because it complicates the initial sales conversation. Bloom discusses it because informed consent requires it, and because the exit strategy should be part of the treatment plan from day one. The moment of pushback: weight regain after discontinuation does not mean the medication "failed." It means obesity is a chronic condition that often requires sustained intervention, and that reality deserves a more honest conversation than it typically gets.
