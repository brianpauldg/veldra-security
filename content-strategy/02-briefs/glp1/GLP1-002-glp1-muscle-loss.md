## The Angle
Every GLP-1 provider talks about weight loss. Almost none address that 30-40% of weight lost on GLP-1 medications may be lean mass. This is not a minor footnote — it is the central challenge of pharmacological weight management. Bloom leads with this conversation because it separates providers who understand body composition from those who only track scale weight. The mitigation strategies (resistance training, protein optimization, monitoring) are where clinical oversight earns its value.
