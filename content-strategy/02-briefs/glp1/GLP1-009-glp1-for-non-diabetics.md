## The Angle
GLP-1 medications were developed for type 2 diabetes, but the FDA weight management approvals (Wegovy 2021, Zepbound 2023) changed the clinical landscape entirely. Most content either treats the obesity indication as secondary or ignores the diabetes origins altogether. The eligibility criteria matter more than the label history. The moment of pushback: not every person who wants to lose weight is a candidate for GLP-1 therapy. The clinical threshold exists for a reason, and providers who prescribe based on desire rather than medical necessity are doing patients a disservice.
