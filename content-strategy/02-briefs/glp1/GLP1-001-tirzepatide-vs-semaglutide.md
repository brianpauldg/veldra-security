## The Angle
The comparison everyone searches for, but most articles get wrong because they cherry-pick trial data without acknowledging cross-trial comparison limitations. STEP and SURMOUNT were different trials with different populations, different endpoints, and different dosing schedules. Placing their results side by side as though they were head-to-head data is intellectually dishonest. This piece explains why the mechanism difference matters, what the data actually supports, and where the honest answer is "we need more direct comparison data."
