## The Angle

"Legal" is the wrong framing — and that's not pedantry, it's a distinction that changes everything about how patients should think about peptide access. No peptide was "made illegal" in the way people assume. The question is which peptides can be compounded under current FDA enforcement posture through authorized compounding pathways, and that's a more nuanced question with a more useful answer. This piece reframes the conversation from legality to compounding eligibility, explains the current state of play, and gives patients the framework to evaluate claims they encounter.
