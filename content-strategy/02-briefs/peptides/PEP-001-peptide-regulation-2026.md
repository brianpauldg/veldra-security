## The Angle

Most peptide content falls into two camps: breathless panic ("peptides are banned") or willful denial ("nothing changed, business as usual"). Both are wrong. The reality is a multi-phase regulatory process that began in late 2023, accelerated through 2024-2025, and will continue to reshape the compounding landscape over the next 12-24 months. This piece walks through the timeline, the mechanism, and the implications without hysteria or minimization.
