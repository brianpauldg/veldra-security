## The Angle
This is the definitive, quarterly-updated regulatory reference for peptide compounding in the United States. It replaces the fragmented, often outdated, and commercially biased regulatory information patients and practitioners currently rely on. The regulatory map covers every peptide in the Bloom Peptide Library plus the broader compounding landscape, organized by FDA category status, 503A/503B applicability, and state-level variance. The quarterly update commitment is the moat — static regulatory content decays within months. Bloom's commitment to maintaining this resource is the differentiator.

This piece is designed to be the link-bait masterpiece of the peptide pillar. It earns inbound links from practitioners, patient communities, and regulatory-adjacent publications because no comparable resource exists.
