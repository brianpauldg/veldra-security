## The Angle
Most symptom listicles recycle the same bullet points without addressing the clinical elephant in the room: reference ranges. A man at 460 ng/dL sits inside the "normal" window while experiencing every textbook symptom of hypogonadism. A man at 320 ng/dL gets flagged and treated. Both feel the same. This piece interrogates the 450 ng/dL paradox — the gap between statistical normality and symptomatic optimization — which is something Hone Health and Henry Meds gloss over because it complicates their funnel. Bloom addresses it head-on because it is the clinical reality.
