## The Angle
Every TRT cost article online quotes a range ($100-$500/month) without explaining what drives the variance. The cheapest options omit labs, monitoring, HCG, and estrogen management — the things that make TRT safe over years, not just weeks. The most expensive options bundle unnecessary concierge fees. This piece breaks down line-item cost components so men can compare apples to apples. Bloom's $199/month add-on bundles compounded testosterone cypionate, HCG, and anastrozole from a licensed 503A pharmacy — and the $49 Optimization Consultation is credited toward Month 1. Transparency is the angle. Most competitors make you schedule a call before they show you a price.
