## The Angle
The testosterone booster market is a multi-billion-dollar industry built largely on weak evidence, proprietary blends, and men who want to avoid needles. This piece does not dismiss natural optimization — some interventions have genuine evidence behind them. But it draws a clear, honest line between what lifestyle and supplementation can do (modest, ceiling-limited improvement in men with correctable deficits) and what TRT does (restore testosterone to target range with predictable, dose-dependent outcomes). The contrarian take: some natural strategies work, just not the ones being marketed to you on Amazon. And when they do work, they work for reasons the supplement companies do not explain.
