## The Angle
After the FDA reclassified HCG as a biologic in 2020, compounding pharmacies faced new regulatory hurdles. Many telehealth clinics quietly dropped HCG from their protocols to simplify supply chains and reduce cost. They framed it as optional. It is not optional — it is a core component of HPTA preservation, testicular maintenance, and fertility protection. Bloom bundles HCG into every TRT protocol because removing it was never a clinical decision. It was a business decision that other companies made at the patient's expense.
