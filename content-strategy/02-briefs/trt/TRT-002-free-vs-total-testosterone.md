## The Angle
Most men walk out of their doctor's office with a single number: total testosterone. It is an incomplete picture. SHBG — sex hormone-binding globulin — sequesters a large fraction of circulating testosterone, rendering it biologically inert. Two men with identical total T can have vastly different free T depending on SHBG, albumin binding, and metabolic context. Most telehealth clinics skip this because testing free T by equilibrium dialysis costs more and complicates the narrative. Bloom tests both because treating the number without understanding bioavailability is guesswork.
