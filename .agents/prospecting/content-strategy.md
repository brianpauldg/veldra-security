## Email Nurture Sequence (New Leads)

**Trigger:** Any lead magnet download or quiz completion
**Sequence:** 5 emails over 14 days

### Email 1 — Immediate (Day 0)
**Subject:** Your results are in
**Content:** Deliver lead magnet results/download. Brief intro to Bloom Metabolics. No hard sell. "Over the next week, I'll send you some of the most useful resources we've put together on [topic]. Reply if you have questions — I read every one."

### Email 2 — Day 2
**Subject:** what "normal" really means
**Content:** The #1 insight — normal lab ranges ≠ optimal. Brief version of blog #6. Include lab decoder image. "If your doctor told you everything looks fine but you don't feel fine, you're not imagining it."

### Email 3 — Day 5
**Subject:** Jason lost 28 lbs in 12 weeks
**Content:** Patient story (Jason R., GLP-1). Real numbers, real timeline, real challenges. Social proof builds trust. Soft CTA: "Curious if you'd be a candidate? [Take the screener]"

### Email 4 — Day 9
**Subject:** the question everyone asks
**Content:** Address the top objection for their segment. For TRT: "Is this safe?" For GLP-1: "Will I gain it back?" For peptides: "Is this legal?" Link to relevant blog post. Position Bloom Metabolics's approach. CTA: "Book a consultation — no obligation, just answers."

### Email 5 — Day 14
**Subject:** ready when you are
**Content:** Direct but not pushy. "You've done the research. You know something needs to change. The next step is a conversation with a licensed provider who can look at your specific situation." Clear consultation booking CTA. "No pressure. No commitment beyond the visit. Just clarity."
