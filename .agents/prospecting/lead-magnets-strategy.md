## Lead Magnet 1: "Is It Low T?" — Hormone Health Self-Assessment Quiz

### Format & Stage
- **Format:** Interactive quiz (12 questions)
- **Buyer stage:** Awareness → Consideration
- **Effort:** Medium (1–2 weeks to build)

### Why It Works
Men Google symptoms before they Google solutions. A quiz validates their suspicion that something's off — and positions Bloom Metabolics as the answer in the same moment. Quizzes also naturally segment leads by symptom severity.

### Content Outline

**Quiz Questions (scored 0–3 per question):**

1. How would you rate your energy levels over the past 3 months? (High / Normal / Noticeably lower / Consistently exhausted)
2. Have you experienced changes in body composition despite consistent diet/exercise? (No change / Minor / Noticeable fat gain or muscle loss / Significant)
3. How would you describe your mental clarity and focus? (Sharp / Occasionally foggy / Frequently foggy / Severe brain fog daily)
4. Rate your libido compared to 2–3 years ago (Same / Slightly lower / Noticeably lower / Significantly decreased)
5. How is your sleep quality? (Great / Decent / Disrupted / Poor — waking frequently or unrestorative)
6. How is your mood and motivation? (Stable / Occasional dips / Frequently low / Persistent apathy or irritability)
7. How well do you recover from exercise? (Quick recovery / Normal / Slower than expected / Very slow or persistent soreness)
8. Age range? (Under 30 / 30–35 / 36–42 / 43–50 / 50+)
9. Have you had your testosterone levels checked in the last 2 years? (Yes, normal / Yes, borderline / Yes, low / No)
10. Have you noticed decreased morning erections? (No change / Occasional / Frequent / Consistent decrease)
11. Do you experience afternoon energy crashes? (Rarely / Sometimes / Most days / Daily)
12. Family history of hormonal issues or early andropause? (No / Unsure / Yes)

**Scoring:**
- 0–10: Low risk — educational content track
- 11–20: Moderate risk — consideration content track + consultation soft-sell
- 21–36: High risk — direct consultation CTA + priority follow-up

**Results Page:**
- Personalized score with interpretation
- "What this means" explanation (compliant, no diagnosis)
- Recommended next step based on score tier
- CTA: "Book a consultation to get your labs checked" (high score) or "Download our free hormone health guide" (low score)

### Gating Strategy
- **Pre-quiz:** Email only (to deliver results)
- **Post-results:** Name + phone for high scorers who want consultation
- Conversion estimate: 40–60% email capture, 15–25% of high scorers book

### Distribution Plan
- Homepage popup (exit-intent): "Wondering if your testosterone is low? Take the 2-minute quiz"
- Blog post CTAs on symptom-related articles
- Meta ads targeting men 35–55 with interest in fitness/health
- Instagram Stories with swipe-up
- Google Ads on "low testosterone symptoms" keywords

### Follow-Up Trigger
- **High score (21+):** Immediate email with results + "Book consultation" CTA → 3-email urgency sequence over 7 days
- **Medium score (11–20):** Results + educational content → 5-email nurture sequence over 21 days
- **Low score (0–10):** Results + hormone health guide → monthly newsletter
