## 4. Overall Assessment

### Positives
- Exact name "Bloom Metabolics" is completely unused — no businesses, trademarks, or social media accounts
- Full domain availability across 7 TLDs is rare
- Compound phrase is distinctive enough to trademark
- "Metabolic" signals clinical credibility; "Blooms" adds warmth, transformation, growth
- Gender-neutral — works for both men and women

### Concerns
- BloomMD is a near-identical competitor in telehealth/GLP-1 — brand confusion risk
- "Bloom" root is oversaturated in wellness branding — SEO competition
- "Blooms" (plural) may read as floral/gardening to some audiences before medical context
- For the 35-55 male demographic seeking TRT, "Blooms" may skew feminine (subjective)
