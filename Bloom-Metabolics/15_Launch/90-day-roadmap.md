# 90-Day Scale Roadmap

---

## Month 2 (Days 45-74): Scale Operations

### Growth
- Expand to 10+ states (additional provider licenses)
- Scale Google Ads to $150-200/day if unit economics hold
- Launch Meta Ads (Instagram, Facebook) for brand awareness + retargeting
- Publish 2 blog posts/week (SEO compounding)
- 10+ referral partners active
- Patient referral program generating 10-15% of new patients

### Clinical
- 3+ providers covering 15+ states
- Standardized clinical protocol documentation (provider training materials)
- Quarterly lab reminders automated for all patients
- Provider peer review process established
- Adverse event reporting procedures tested and documented

### Operations
- 100+ active patients
- Automate refill processing (auto-request → pharmacy → ship, with provider override for flags)
- Implement advanced analytics dashboard (cohort analysis, retention curves)
- Hire first operations coordinator (part-time or contract)
- Weekly team standup + monthly business review established

### Technology
- Advanced patient portal (EHR-integrated, mobile-friendly)
- Implement feedback surveys (NPS at Day 30, 60, 90)
- SEO improvements based on Search Console data
- Website performance optimization (Core Web Vitals)

**Targets:** 100+ active patients, $20,000-30,000 MRR, 3+ providers, <5% churn

---

## Month 3 (Days 75-104): Optimize & Expand

### Growth
- 150-200+ active patients
- Explore insurance coordination for branded GLP-1 (Wegovy, Zepbound) — high-margin upsell
- Launch programmatic SEO (city-specific pages: "TRT telehealth in [city]")
- Explore podcast sponsorships or health influencer partnerships
- Consider Series Seed fundraising if metrics warrant it ($500K-1M for accelerated growth)
- Revenue goal: $25,000-50,000 MRR

### Clinical
- 5+ providers covering 20+ states
- Peptide therapy fully operational with established protocols
- Clinical outcome tracking (pre/post lab comparison for TRT, weight tracking for GLP-1)
- Publish first outcome data (anonymized, compliance-approved)
- Explore GLP-1 branded medication access via insurance coordination

### Operations
- Full-time operations coordinator
- Customer support SLA: <4 hours for clinical, <24 hours for billing
- Churn reduction initiatives based on 60+ days of data
- Vendor renegotiations (pharmacy pricing, lab pricing based on volume)
- HIPAA Security Risk Assessment completed (annual requirement)

### Technology
- Mobile app consideration (or progressive web app)
- Implement AI-powered protocol recommendations (provider approves, AI suggests based on similar patient profiles)
- Advanced reporting: LTV by cohort, CAC by channel, treatment outcome correlations
- API integrations with pharmacy (automated ordering) and lab partner (automated results)

### Business Development
- Explore white-label/B2B opportunities (other clinics using our platform)
- Explore corporate wellness partnerships (employer-sponsored metabolic health)
- Attend 1-2 telehealth industry events or conferences
- Build advisory board (physician, compliance expert, growth marketer)

**Targets:** 150-200+ patients, $35,000-50,000+ MRR, 5+ providers, 20+ states, <4% churn
