## FAQ Preview

**Q: Do I need to see a doctor in person?**
A: No. All consultations are conducted via secure video with a licensed provider. Lab work is done at a Quest or Labcorp location near you — no appointment needed, just walk in.

**Q: Is this covered by insurance?**
A: Bloom Metabolics operates on a cash-pay model. We do not bill insurance directly. However, you may be able to submit receipts to your insurance or use HSA/FSA funds. Check with your plan for eligibility.

**Q: How quickly can I start treatment?**
A: Most patients go from consultation to medication delivery within 7-10 days, depending on lab turnaround and pharmacy processing. We prioritize getting you started safely and efficiently.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel, pause, or change your plan at any time from your account. Cancellation is effective at the end of your current billing period.

**Q: Are the medications FDA-approved?**
A: We prescribe both FDA-approved and compounded medications depending on your treatment plan. Compounded medications are prepared by licensed pharmacies based on your provider's prescription. Your provider will discuss the specific medications recommended for you during your consultation.
