## Partner Onboarding Kit

When a partner signs, provide:
1. **Referral link** (UTM-tagged: `?utm_source=partner&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign={partnerName}`)
2. **Physical referral cards** (business card size with QR code to assessment)
3. **Talking points** (1-page guide: what to say when a client/member asks about symptoms)
4. **Co-branded landing page** (partner logo + Bloom Metabolics, customized messaging)
5. **Monthly report template** (referrals sent, converted, earnings)
6. **Partner agreement** (simple 1-page: referral fee, payment terms, compliance requirements)

---

## Compliance Notes for Partnerships
- Partners must NOT make medical claims or diagnoses
- Partners should say: "I work with Bloom Metabolics — they have licensed physicians who specialize in this. Take their free assessment."
- Partners must NOT receive PHI about their referred patients
- All referral marketing must follow FTC guidelines
- Anti-kickback considerations: consult attorney to ensure referral fees are structured compliantly (Stark Law exemptions for non-Medicare/Medicaid may apply, but verify)
